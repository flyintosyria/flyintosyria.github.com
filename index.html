Syria needs you!

Our observers, both professional and untrained volunteers, will arrive in Syria as individual civilians, at a time of their choosing, and for any duration of time as they see fit.

Their mission, once on Syrian soil, will be to report on the events around them, give personal accounts, and provide any sort of humanitarian aid they are capable of.

The motivation behind this organization is to assist the UN in its worthy effort to shed light on the Syrian crisis and provide much needed attention and assistance.

We believe, much as the UN does, that substantial international presence in Syria will have a pacifying effect on the clashing sides, and that any added media attention will lead to higher diplomatic pressures on all relevant sides to end the hostilities.

In addition to all of the above, and though it’s hard to tolerate the sights and sounds coming out of Syria with every passing day, we wish to see a non-violent movement to end for the crisis, one which will deliver the right message to future generations and leaders in Syria and the region.

It is our sincere hope that you will support our organization with moral backing, professional guidance, equipment, and civilian personnel.
